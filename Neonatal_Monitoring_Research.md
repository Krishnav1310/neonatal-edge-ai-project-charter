# Neonatal Monitoring Research

## Current Scenario

Newborn babies admitted to the NICU are continuously monitored using bedside patient monitors. These monitors display vital signs such as Heart Rate, SpO₂, Respiratory Rate, and Temperature.

Doctors and nurses observe these values manually to identify any abnormal conditions.

## Existing Challenges

- Continuous manual monitoring is difficult.
- Early signs of deterioration may be missed.
- Staff workload is high.
- Existing systems generate threshold-based alarms but do not predict future risks.

## Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-assisted neonatal monitoring system capable of analyzing vital signs in real time and providing early risk prediction while keeping clinicians in control of medical decisions.
